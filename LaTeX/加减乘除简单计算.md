$$
\frac{53 \times 3+57 \times 4}{7} \approx 55.29
$$

$$
bjSumA=55.29 \times 7=53 \times 3+57 \times 4=387
$$

$$
bjSumB=35.14*7=245.98
$$

$$
\frac{bjSumB + x \times 3}{10} > \frac{bjSumA + 86.6 \times 3}{10} \implies x>133.6
$$
